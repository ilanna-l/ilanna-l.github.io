Hi! On this page you will find a playable window of my Final project for my CSC 106 class along with a video of me explaiing the code.
For the project, my partner and I chose to create a simplified version of Blackjack. The game includes a splash screen with a Start button
that will bring you to the game and a How To button that will bring you to an instruction screen on how to play the game. In the game
the player and dealer are dealt their first cards just like how a normal game would. The player than has the option to continue to "Hit"
in order to add more cards to their hand or choose "Stand" which will end their turn and allow the dealer to hit based on the player score
and how close they are to 21. Whoever is closest to 21 without going over wins!
